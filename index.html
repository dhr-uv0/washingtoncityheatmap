SMB Definition (Industry Standard for Exit / M&A Advisory)
Employees 5–249 · Revenue proxy $500K–$50M · Sectors: manufacturing, wholesale, retail, food service, healthcare, professional services, construction, logistics (NAICS 23, 31–33, 42, 44–45, 48–49, 62, 72).
Tier 3 Target Zone Rationale
Equal weighting of population size, median household income, and 2010–2020 growth rate (all normalized 0–100). Tier 3 mid-markets (30k–59k pop) are large enough for consistent SMB pipeline, small enough to be underserved by national consulting competitors.
